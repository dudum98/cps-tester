Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 23, 2025

Summary: CPS Tester does not collect personal data on a server. This tool runs entirely in your browser. The only data stored is optional high-score data saved locally in your browser's localStorage so you can see your scores between visits.

What we store: High score numbers (CPS values) are stored in localStorage keyed by the selected mode. No names, emails, or IP addresses are sent to any server by default.

Third-party services: The site uses no analytics, advertising, or third-party trackers by default. If third-party services are added in the future, they will be disclosed here.

Cookies and tracking: The site does not set cookies for tracking. Browser storage is limited to localStorage for high scores.

Contact: If you have privacy questions, contact the site owner at replace-with-your-email@example.com.

We may update this policy from time to time. Check this page for the latest version.
